Apogee Textiles

APOGEE Textiles is a a US-based, full service textile sourcing company that specializes in connecting clients with quality, cost competitive, and reputable factories. Our extensive network of suppliers allows us to accommodate any Textile clothing or accessories line. We offers high-performance flat linen for hotel, hospitality and health-care industries in addition to High End Home Textiles, Furnishing fabrics and Apparels for all age groups. We are one stop solution for all of your textile related sourcing needs.

Our products are designed for longevity. By using excellent yarns and fabrics, we address all corners of your life style with our supreme quality cotton molded into your desired products.

Our corporate culture is based on fairness and openness in our relations with our customers and partners, something which always leads to long-term and very reliable relationships.

For us, sustainability is not a “megatrend”, but deeply rooted in our DNA. At Apogee we work as a team and are committed along the entire value chain to respecting values and assuming responsibility as a good corporate citizen.
